Who Are We

Our Background

Lotus Builders & Developers is a youthful, energetic, and lively real estate developer based in Dombivali-Kalyan, Mumbai. The company was established to fulfill people's aspirations to buy a home.


With a proven track record of quality spanning over 5 years, we are one of the fastest-growing professional real estate businesses in the region.
